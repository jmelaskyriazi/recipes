Ingredients:
	- Ribeye steak
	- Salt
	- Pepper
	- Olive oil

Equipment:
	- Charcoal grill
	- Charcoal

Instructions:

1. Marinate the steak in salt, pepper, and olive oil for 30 mins
2. Put the steak on a hot grill and flip after 5 mins or so
3. Take it off rare, wrap up in tin foil for a few mins to continue cooking
4. Enjoy your steak
